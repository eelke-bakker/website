Street name visualization


Year: 2020

The Street name visualization is an independent research project, inspired by the work of Erin, and Abdishakur. It takes open source data about Dutch streetnames and turns it into a colorful representation of 'streets', 'alleys', 'squares', 'lanes', each representing a different urban structure. It also shows the names of people we have named streets after, and their function. Were they painters, poets, politicians? What is the best profession to choose, if you would like to have a treet named after you?
