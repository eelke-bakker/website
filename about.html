I'm a creative and curious

This is me. I am currently pursuing a professional doctorate in Data Science at the Jheronimus Academy of Data Science in the Netherlands. My hope for this website is that it reflects my personality and the diversity and curiosity that I hope my work illustrate. My main interests are in using Data Science for Social Good and humanitarian action, to create products which do not only make computers 'think', but which make humans think as well.
